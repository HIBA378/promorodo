What is the Pomodoro technique?

The Pomodoro technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It involves using a timer to divide work into 25-minute intervals, called "Pomodoros", separated by short 5-minute breaks. After 4 Pomodoros, a longer break of 15-30 minutes is recommended to maximize concentration and productivity.

Who uses the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is used by people who need to focus on their activities to achieve certain goals. Many of your favorite YouTubers, influencers and entrepreneurs are likely to use this technique to be more productive. If you find yourself easily distracted and procrastinating, this may be your antidote.
